Our 5W1H:

Who: The Project 5W1H team is not just one person. We are a team who met in college and now want to create a connection with our generation, right as our "adult" lives start and we regret not pursuing spontaneous ideas.

What: The main focus is to start a dialogue among people. Through photography and social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook, the featured interviews could help anyone feel less alone and connected with our stories.

When: We have started presenting stories in 2017. Hopefully we can continue for years to come with your support!

Where: Arizona based but we come from various backgrounds.

Why: In high school, there was a history teacher named Mr. Tribby. One of the lectures he gave told us it was easy to get lost in the crowd. Do something that makes you stand out of the crowd.

How: Taking pictures, posting them on Insta/FB and getting a bunch of likes is cool. Creating content with feeling and substance that could help a teenager fighting through depression feel relevant & connected by somone's story who is only a couple years older than them, that's cooler.
